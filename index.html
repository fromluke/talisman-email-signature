Email Signature Setup

Gmail

  1. Generate and copy the output/result from the email signature builder.
  2. Within Gmail, click the Settings gear icon, then click “See all settings”.
  3. Scroll down to the Signature section. Paste the entire signature, being sure to maintain type weights and styling.
  4. Click “Save Changes” at the bottom of the screen.

Apple Mail

  1. Generate and copy the output/result from the email signature builder.
  2. Within Mac Mail, go to Mail > Preferences, and click the Signatures tab.
  3. Uncheck the “Always match my default message font” below the form field.
  4. Paste the entire signature, being sure to maintain type weights and styling.
  5. If it’s not already selected, under “Choose Signature”, select the signature just created.
  6. Done. No save button. Compose a new message and make sure the formatting is correct.
